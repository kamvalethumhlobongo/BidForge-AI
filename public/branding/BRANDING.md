# BidForge AI Branding Guidelines

## Visual Identity
BidForge AI is a premium, corporate tool designed for professionals. The visual identity should reflect trust, efficiency, and cutting-edge technology.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** `#1E3A8A` (Deep Blue) - Represents trust and stability.
- **Accent Gold:** `#D4AF37` - Represents winning and premium quality.
- **Neutral Charcoal:** `#374151` - For text and professional contrast.
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF` - For clean, open space.
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` - For positive status and wins.

## Typography
- **Headings:** `Montserrat` or `Playfair Display` (Semi-bold to Bold).
- **Body:** `Inter` or `Roboto` (Regular to Medium).
- **Mono:** `JetBrains Mono` for any code or data snippets.

## Assets
- **Logo:** `logo.png` (Minimalist forge/document abstract).
- **Hero Banner:** `hero_banner.png` (Premium corporate hero banner).
- **Icons:**
    - `icons/bid_icon.png` (Document icon with AI sparkle).
    - `icons/success_icon.png` (Premium gold checkmark).
    - `icons/favicon.png` (Stylized 'B' favicon).
- **Dashboard Mockup:** `dashboard_mockup.html` (Interactive HTML/Tailwind mockup).
- **Dashboard Screenshot:** `dashboard_screenshot.png` (Visual reference for the dashboard).

- Professional
