# BidForge AI — Personalized Bid Snippet Workflow

## Overview
When a lead shows interest (replied to a DM, opened an email, or requested more info), the **Prompt Expert** produces a **Personalized Bid Snippet** within **2 hours**. This is undeniable proof of quality — a real sample of what their proposal would look like.

---

## How To Request A Snippet

Send the **Prompt Expert** a message with these details:

```
LEAD: [Company Name]
INDUSTRY: [logistics | construction | security | cleaning]
TENDER: [Name of tender they're targeting]
SECTION: [executiveSummary | methodology]       ← optional, defaults to executiveSummary
TIER: [standard | advanced | premium]           ← optional, defaults to advanced
DETAILS:
  - Vehicle count: [if logistics]
  - Years in business: [any industry]
  - Certifications: [e.g. ISO 9001, NHVAS]
  - Key strength: [one-line differentiator]
```

---

## What You Get Back

A ready-to-send markdown document with:

1. **Header banner** — company name, tender, tier, section
2. **Professional proposal excerpt** — evaluator-ready content
3. **Footer CTA** — "This is a sample of what your full proposal would look like..."

---

## Where To Send It

| Lead Stage | Best Channel |
|---|---|
| Replied to LinkedIn DM | LinkedIn message |
| Opened cold email | Reply to same email thread |
| Asked for sample | Direct email with subject: "Your personalized bid snippet — [Company Name]" |

---

## Example Request → Response

**Your request:**
```
LEAD: Sydney Logistics Co
INDUSTRY: logistics
TENDER: Transport NSW — Regional Freight Services
TIER: premium
DETAILS: 45 vehicles, 12 years, ISO 9001+NHVAS
```

**You receive:** A premium-tier Executive Summary for Sydney Logistics Co, personalized for their Transport NSW tender. Ready to paste into an email or LinkedIn message.
