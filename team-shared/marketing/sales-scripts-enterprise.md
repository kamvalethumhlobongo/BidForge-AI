# Sales Scripts — Closing SMEs on the Bid Desk (Premium DFY) Plan

## Overview
The **Bid Desk** (Premium DFY / Done-For-You) plan targets SMEs bidding on **large government tenders** — typically R500K+ contracts with complex multi-part RFPs. These customers need expert oversight and submission assistance.

**Price point:** R15,000 – R50,000+ per submission
**Decision-maker:** Owner/Director/CEO
**Pain point:** Complex tenders that are too big for DIY but too small for a R50K bid writer

---

## Script 1: Discovery Call (15 min)

### Opening (0–2 min)
> "Thanks for taking the call, [Name]. I know you're busy, so I'll be direct: BidForge AI helps SMEs win government contracts by producing professional tender responses fast. Before I dive into what we do, can you tell me — what's your biggest frustration with the tender process right now?"

### Discovery Questions (2–10 min)
**Key questions to identify need for Bid Desk tier:**
1. "What kind of tenders are you going after — and what's the typical contract value?"
2. "How are you currently handling tender responses — in-house, external writers, or DIY?"
3. "What's your biggest blocker: time, writing quality, compliance requirements, or something else?"
4. "How many evaluation criteria does a typical RFP have — and how confident are you that you're hitting all of them?"
5. "Have you ever lost a tender that you felt you should have won? What happened?"
6. "How much are you currently spending on bid writing or proposal development per tender?"

### Qualifying for Bid Desk (10–12 min)
**Signs this lead needs Bid Desk tier:**
- Tenders valued at $500K+
- Multi-part RFPs with 30+ evaluation criteria
- Current submissions getting rejected at compliance gate
- "We've tried AI tools but they weren't detailed enough"
- They mention needing "human oversight" or "expert review"

### Transition to Solution (12–15 min)
> "Based on what you've described, I think our Bid Desk tier would be the perfect fit. Here's why: [reference their specific pain points].
>
> With Bid Desk, you get:
> - A senior bid writer reviewing your submission
> - Full compliance matrix mapping — so you know you haven't missed anything
> - Submission assistance — formatting, uploading, checking
> - Unlimited revisions until you're happy
>
> The result: a submission that evaluators take seriously. No compliance fails. No 'close but no cigar.'
>
> The investment is [custom quote]. Compared to [their current cost / cost of losing], how does that sit with you?"

### Handling Objections

**Objection: "That's expensive"**
> "I get it. Let me ask — what did the last tender you lost cost you in potential revenue? If this investment increases your win rate by even one contract, what's the ROI? For most of our clients, one win covers the cost 10x over."

**Objection: "We need to think about it"**
> "Absolutely. When does the RFP close? I want to make sure we have enough time to deliver a quality response. If you're comfortable, I can draft a scope of work for the submission and send it over — no pressure to proceed until you've reviewed it."

**Objection: "Can your AI really handle complex tenders?"**
> "Great question. Our AI is trained on your industry's specific requirements, and every Bid Desk submission is reviewed by a human expert before it reaches you. Think of it as AI-powered efficiency with human-level quality control. You get the best of both."

### Close (15 min)
> "Here's what I suggest: Send me the RFP and I'll have our team do a free Compliance Gap Analysis — we'll identify which criteria your current capability covers and where you need to strengthen. No obligation. If you like what you see, we move forward on Bid Desk. Deal?"

## Script 2: Follow-up Call (After Sending Proposal/Quote)

### Opening
> "Hi [Name], thanks for taking my call. I sent over the scope and pricing for your [Tender Name] submission. What questions came to mind when you reviewed it?"

### Three-Step Close
**Step 1 — Recap Value:**
> "Just to recap, for this submission you'll get: a full compliance matrix, written responses to every evaluation criterion, a professional PDF ready for upload, and a senior reviewer checking everything before delivery. All within your RFP deadline."

**Step 2 — Address Risk:**
> "I want to make this completely risk-free for you. If we deliver the submission and you genuinely feel it doesn't improve your chances — you don't pay. Fair?"

**Step 3 — Ask for Commitment:**
> "To get started, I just need the RFP PDF and your company profile. I can send you a simple intake link. Should we move forward?"

### If They Stall
> "I respect that you want to be thorough. Can I ask — what's the one thing holding you back? Is it budget, trust, timing, or something else?"

Then address directly.

### If They Say Yes
> "Excellent. Let me send you the intake link right now. I'll also CC our senior bid reviewer so you know exactly who's working on your submission. You're in good hands."

## Key Closing Principles

1. **Always tie back to their pain** — "You said losing tenders at compliance is your biggest frustration. Our Bid Desk tier eliminates that risk."
2. **Price anchor high** — Start at R30K, justify value, then flex if needed.
3. **Risk reversal** — "If you don't feel it improves your chance, you don't pay."
4. **Urgency** — "The RFP closes on [date]. We need to start by [date] to guarantee quality."
5. **Social proof** — "We recently did a Bid Desk submission for [similar company] and they [result]."
