# 7-Day "First Customer" Outreach Strategy

## Overview
**Goal:** Drive first paying customer (Single Proposal Pack or Winning Bid Package) within 7 days
**Target Audience:** SME owners/directors in Logistics, Construction, Security, Cleaning, and Gov Supply in Australia.
**Core Channels:** LinkedIn (owner outreach) + Cold Email (targeted sequences)
**Budget:** $0 (organic) — can scale with LinkedIn Premium/Sales Navigator if available

---

## Day 1 — Launch Day: "The Warm Inbound"
### Action:
1. **Post Launch Announcement on LinkedIn** (see Social Media Posts doc)
2. **Post in 3–5 SME/industry Facebook Groups** (e.g., "Australian Transport & Logistics Owners", "Small Business Tenders Australia")
3. **Email existing network** — send personal invites to 10 past colleagues/contacts offering free beta access in exchange for testimonial

### Metrics:
- Inbound link clicks from LinkedIn post
- Group comments & DM enquiries

---

## Day 2 — LinkedIn Outreach: Logistics & Transport
### Target List (find via LinkedIn search):
- Operations Directors / Fleet Managers at 10–15 transport SMEs
- Keywords: "logistics director", "transport company owner", "fleet manager Australia"

### Connection Request Template:
> Hi [Name], I came across your profile and see you're in transport/logistics. We just launched BidForge AI — we help logistics SMEs win government and corporate tenders with professional proposals delivered in 24h. Would love to connect.

### Follow-up Message (after connection accepted):
> Thanks for connecting, [Name]! Quick question: how are you currently handling tender responses and capability statements? We've built BidForge AI specifically for transport operators who need fast, professional bid documentation. If you'd like, I can generate a free capability statement for your business — no obligation. Interested?

### Metrics:
- Connection request acceptance rate (target: 40%+)
- Follow-up conversations booked

---

### Source emails from:
- Company websites (About/Contact pages)
- Industry directories (Master Builders Australia, ASIAL for security)
- ABR lookup

>
> Our turnaround: **24 hours.**
> Our industries: Construction, Security, Logistics, Cleaning, Gov Supply.
>
> Want to give it a shot? Just reply with the RFP or a brief description — we'll handle the rest.

### Metrics:
- Open rate (target: 55%+)
- Reply rate (target: 10%+)
- Conversion to free trial (target: 3–5)

## Day 6 — Follow-ups & Demo Offers
### Action:
1. **Follow up on ALL conversations** from Days 2–5
2. Send a "quick check-in" email to non-responders:
   > **Subject:** Still interested in free AI-generated bids?
   >
   > Hi [Name], just following up on my email from earlier this week. Our free first-bid offer is still open — I'd love to help you win your next tender. No strings attached.
   >
   > Best,
   > [Name]

3. For hot leads: Offer a **15-min Zoom demo** showing how fast it works
4. Record a 90-second Loom video showing a real-time bid generation to share with prospects

### Metrics:
- Demo requests booked
- Active trial users onboarded

---

## Day 7 — Close & Celebrate First Customer
### Action:
1. **Convert trial users to paying customers** — offer 30% off first Winning Bid Package
2. **Close at least 1 paying customer** from the pipeline
3. **Capture testimonial** from first customer (video or text)
4. **Post "First Customer" win on LinkedIn** (shows social proof)
5. **Review and refine** — which channel (LinkedIn vs Email) drove the best results? Double down.

### Celebrate:
- Share the win with the team
- Collect feedback for product improvement
- Document the exact outreach sequence that worked

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Day 1–3 Target | Day 7 Target |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn connections sent | 30 | 75 |
| Cold emails sent | 50 | 150 |
| Replies / conversations | 5 | 15 |
| Free trial signups | 3 | 10 |
| Paying customers | 0 | 1–3 |
| Revenue | R0 | R750–R7,500 |

## Key Success Factors
1. **Personalisation wins** — Customise every message with the prospect's name and company
2. **Speed of response** — Reply within minutes when a lead responds
3. **Offer clear value** — "Free first bid" removes all risk
4. **Follow up persistently** — Most sales happen on the 3rd–5th touchpoint
