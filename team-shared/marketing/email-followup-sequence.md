## Email 2 — Value Add + Tips (Day 3, if not yet submitted)

**Subject:** 3 tips to write a winning tender response

Hi [First Name],

I know starting a new tender response can feel overwhelming. Here are 3 things our best-performing users do:

**1️⃣ Address every evaluation criterion directly**
Evaluators use scoring matrices. If your response doesn't explicitly address a criterion, you get zero points. Our AI does this automatically — but it helps if you flag the key criteria upfront.

**2️⃣ Use evidence, not adjectives**
Instead of "we provide excellent service," write "we've maintained a 98% on-time delivery rate across 200+ contracts." Numbers win tenders.

**3️⃣ Make the evaluator's job easy**
Clear headings, bolded keywords, short paragraphs. Our documents are formatted for readability — but you can take it further by adding your company's visual branding.

**Ready to try it?** Your free bid is still waiting:
