# BidForge AI — 7-Day First Customer Outreach Plan

**Goal:** Drive first paying customer (Single Proposal Pack or Winning Bid Package) within 7 days
**Focus Pain Point:** SMEs losing contracts due to slow/weak paperwork
**Target:** SME owners/directors in Logistics, Construction, Security, Cleaning, Gov Supply (Australia)

---

## Strategy Overview

### Core Message (Every Outreach)
> "You're losing contracts — not because your service isn't good enough, but because your paperwork is slow, weak, and unconvincing. BidForge AI fixes that in 24 hours."

### Channels (Ranked by Impact)
| Channel | Effort | Expected ROI | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. LinkedIn DM Outreach | Medium | High | Days 2, 4, 6 |
| 2. Cold Email | High | Medium | Days 3, 5 |
| 3. Industry Facebook Groups | Low | Medium | Daily |
| 4. Personal Network | Low | High | Day 1 |

---

## Day-by-Day Execution Plan

### Day 1 — Launch & Warm Intros

**Actions:**
1. Post launch announcement on LinkedIn (see `social-media-posts.md` Post #1)
2. Post in 3–5 industry Facebook groups:
   - "Australian Transport & Logistics Owners"
   - "Small Business Tenders Australia"
   - "Construction Contractors Australia"
   - "Security Business Owners Australia"
3. Send **10 personal emails** to past contacts/colleagues:
   > Hey [Name], we just launched BidForge AI — helping SMEs win tenders with professional bid docs delivered in 24h. I'd love your feedback. Want a free bid? Just send me an RFP and I'll generate it for you.

**Success Metric:** 5 replies, 3 free trial signups

---

### Day 2 — LinkedIn Outreach Wave 1 (Logistics & Transport)

**Target:** Operations Directors / Fleet Managers at 15 transport SMEs
**Search terms on LinkedIn:** "logistics director", "transport company owner", "fleet manager Australia"

**Connection Request Template:**
> Hi [Name], I came across your profile. We just launched BidForge AI — helping transport SMEs win government & corporate tenders with professional proposals delivered in 24h. Would love to connect.

**Follow-up (after accepted):**
> Thanks [Name]! Quick question — how are you currently handling tender responses? We built BidForge AI specifically for transport operators who need fast, professional bid documentation. Happy to generate a free capability statement for your business — no obligation. Interested?

**Success Metric:** 40%+ acceptance rate, 5 follow-up conversations

---

>

### Day 4 — LinkedIn Outreach Wave 2 (Security & Cleaning)

**Target:** Security company directors, cleaning business owners targeting commercial contracts

**Message:**

We built BidForge AI specifically for businesses like yours — it generates professional tender responses in 24h so you never lose a contract to weak paperwork. Would you be open to a 5-min chat to see if it could help?

**Success Metric:** 15 connection requests sent, 3 conversations started

---

### Day 6 — Follow-up Blitz

**Actions:**
1. Follow up on ALL conversations from Days 2–5 within 2 hours of any reply
2. Send re-engagement email to non-responders:
   > **Subject:** Still interested in free AI-generated bids?
   > Hi [Name], just following up. Our free first-bid offer is still open. No strings attached. Want to give it a shot?
   > Best, [Name]
3. For hot leads: offer **15-min Zoom demo** showing live bid generation
4. Record a **90-second Loom video** demonstrating the platform for prospects

**Success Metric:** 3 demo requests, 2 active trial users

---

### Day 7 — Close

**Actions:**
1. Convert trial users to paying customers — offer **30% off first Winning Bid Package**
2. Close at least **1 paying customer** from the pipeline
3. Capture testimonial from first customer
4. Post "First Customer Win" on LinkedIn for social proof
5. Review: which channel (LinkedIn vs Email) drove best results? Double down.

**Success Metric:** 1–3 paying customers, R750–R7,500 in revenue

## Key Success Factors

1. **Personalise every message** — Name, company, industry reference
2. **Speed wins** — Reply within minutes when a lead responds
3. **Risk-free offer** — "Free first bid" removes all objections
4. **Follow up persistently** — Most sales happen on 3rd–5th touch
5. **Collect testimonials early** — Social proof accelerates everything
