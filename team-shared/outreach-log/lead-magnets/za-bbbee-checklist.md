# ZA Tender-Ready Checklist — South African Market
## B-BBEE & Local Compliance Essentials for SA SMEs

### B-BBEE Compliance
- [ ] B-BBEE Certificate (current, valid 12 months)
- [ ] B-BBEE Scorecard with all 5 elements:
  - [ ] Ownership (points for black ownership)
  - [ ] Management Control (black representation at board/senior)
  - [ ] Skills Development (training expenditure)
  - [ ] Enterprise & Supplier Development (spend with black SMEs)
  - [ ] Socio-Economic Development (CSI contributions)
- [ ] B-BBEE Level 1-4 (preferred for government tenders)
- [ ] EME/QSE certification if applicable (under R10M/R50M turnover)

### CIDB Grading (Construction & Engineering)
- [ ] CIDB Register certificate
- [ ] Grading designation (1-9 across applicable works categories)
- [ ] Valid annual renewal

### Tax Compliance
- [ ] SARS Tax Clearance Certificate (TCC)
- [ ] Valid PIN on SARS eFiling
- [ ] No outstanding tax disputes

### Industry-Specific
- [ ] PSIRA Registration (Security)
- [ ] SAPS Clearance (Security personnel)
- [ ] ISO 9001 or SABS certification
- [ ] COIDA registration (Compensation Fund)
- [ ] UIF registration
- [ ] Employment Equity Report (annual submission)

### Common Disqualifiers in SA
- Expired B-BBEE certificate
- CIDB grading doesn't match tender class
- Missing tax clearance
- No local content declaration
- Incomplete pricing schedules
