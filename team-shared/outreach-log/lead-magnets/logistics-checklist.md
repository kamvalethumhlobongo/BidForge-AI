# Logistics Tender Compliance Checklist
## Essential Documents for Winning Transport RFPs

### Pre-Submission Checklist
- [ ] NHVAS Accreditation (National Heavy Vehicle Accreditation Scheme)
- [ ] Chain of Responsibility (CoR) Compliance
- [ ] Fatigue Management System Documentation
- [ ] Vehicle Registration & Permits
- [ ] Public Liability Insurance ($10M+)
- [ ] Dangerous Goods Licence (if applicable)
- [ ] ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management
- [ ] ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management
- [ ] Fleet Maintenance Records

### Response Must-Haves
- [ ] Fleet capability statement with vehicle types
- [ ] Driver qualifications and fatigue management plan
- [ ] Fleet tracking and telematics details
- [ ] Maintenance schedule and compliance
- [ ] Incident management procedure
- [ ] Reporting and KPI framework
- [ ] Pricing schedule (per km, per load, or fixed)
