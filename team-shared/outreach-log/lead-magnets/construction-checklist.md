# Construction Tender Compliance Checklist
## Essential Documents for Winning Construction RFPs

### Pre-Submission Checklist
- [ ] Builder's Licence (state-specific)
- [ ] BCA Compliance (Building Code of Australia)
- [ ] SWMS (Safe Work Method Statements)
- [ ] ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management
- [ ] ISO 45001:2018 OH&S Management
- [ ] ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management
- [ ] Public Liability Insurance ($20M+)
- [ ] Workers' Compensation Insurance
- [ ] Project Bank Account Setup (if govt-funded)
- [ ] White Card (all personnel)
- [ ] Relevant Trade Licences

### Response Must-Haves
- [ ] Project methodology and delivery approach
- [ ] Project team structure and qualifications
- [ ] Safety management plan
- [ ] Environmental management plan
- [ ] Quality assurance framework
- [ ] Risk management register
- [ ] Past project case studies (3+)
- [ ] Subcontractor management approach
- [ ] Pricing schedule with rates
