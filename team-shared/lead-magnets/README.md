## Marketing Use Cases

### 1. Tender-Winning Checklists (Lead Magnets)
**Format:** 1-page downloadable PDF  
**Target:** Landing page visitors, email capture  
**CTA:** "Download your free [Industry] Tender-Winning Checklist"  
**Contents per industry:**
- 10-point pre-submission audit with checkbox format
- Evaluation weight framework table
- Bid-winning tips specific to each industry
- CTA to BidForge AI at the bottom

### 2. Capability Statement Framework (Bonus)
**Format:** Multi-page guide + template  
**Target:** Winning Bid Package customers (R3,500–R12,000)  
**Contents:**
- 8-section capability statement structure with fill-in-the-blanks
- Industry-specific content suggestions
- The "47-second test" for self-evaluation
- Complete page layout template
- Strong vs weak differentiator examples

### 3. Bid Desk (DFY) Research Template (Enterprise)
**Format:** Internal use + client intake  
**Target:** High-ticket leads (R15,000–R50,000+)  
**Contents:**
- 12-section comprehensive research template
- Tender overview, compliance, pricing, personnel sections
- Handoff checklist for proposal team
- Post-submission tracking

## How to Use
- **Marketer:** Convert checklists into styled PDFs with company branding for email capture
- **Designer:** Format capability statement guide as a premium PDF with BidForge AI branding
- **Prompt Expert:** Use DFY template when high-ticket leads come in for custom research/response
