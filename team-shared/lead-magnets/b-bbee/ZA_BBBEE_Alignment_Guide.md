## 1. Understanding B-BBEE in Government Tenders

B-BBEE (Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment) is a **competitive scoring factor** that can make or break your tender.

Under the **Preferential Procurement Policy Framework Act (PPPFA)** :

| Contract Value | Score Split | B-BBEE Max |
|---|---|---|
| Up to R1,000,000 | 80 Price : 20 B-BBEE | 20 points |
| Over R1,000,000 | 90 Price : 10 B-BBEE | 10 points |

A Level 1 B-BBEE contributor with the highest price can still beat a Level 8 with the lowest price.

## 3. The 5 B-BBEE Scorecard Elements

### Element 1: Ownership (25 points)
- Exercisable voting rights — Black: 25%+1 vote
- Exercisable voting rights — Black Women: 10%
- Economic interest — Black: 25%
- Economic interest — Black Women: 10%
- New entrants: 2%
**Bonus:** 3pts for 30%+ Black women ownership

### Element 2: Management Control (19 pts / 15 Construction)
- Black Board members: 50%
- Black Executive Management: 60%
- Black Senior Management: 60%
- Black Middle Management: 75%
- Black Junior Management: 80%
**Bonus:** 2pts for 30%+ Black women in executive management

### Element 3: Skills Development (25 pts / 20 Construction)
- Skills spend on Black employees: 6% of leviable amount
- Skills spend on Black women: 3%
- Black people with disabilities: 0.3%
- Learnerships for Black youth: 2.5% of workforce
**Bonus:** 4pts for 2.5%+ absorption into permanent roles

### Element 4: Enterprise & Supplier Development (40 pts / 30 Construction)
- B-BBEE procurement spend: 80% of total procurement
- Spend from QSEs: 15% of total
- Spend from EMEs: 15% of total
- Supplier development: 2% of NPAT
- Enterprise development: 1% of NPAT
**Bonus:** 3pts for 5%+ of NPAT on ED

### Element 5: Socio-Economic Development (5 pts)
- SED contributions: 1% of NPAT

## 5. Quick Wins to Improve Your B-BBEE Level

**Free / Low Cost:**
- Record all procurement from B-BBEE compliant suppliers (immediate)
- Appoint Black-owned service providers for non-core services (immediate)
- Formalise supplier training program (in-kind time, 2 months)

**R15K-R30K:**
- Register employees in learnerships (1-3 months)
- Implement internship program for graduates (1 month)

**Medium Investment:**
- Ownership restructuring to include Black shareholders (3-6 months)
- Promote Black employees into management (1-3 months)
- Formalise SED program at 1% of NPAT

## 7. B-BBEE in Your Tender Response

**Submit with every bid:**
- Valid B-BBEE certificate or sworn affidavit
- B-BBEE scorecard (full if generic, for tenders over R10M)
- CSD summary showing B-BBEE level

**Presentation tips:**
1. State your B-BBEE level prominently on pricing section first page
2. Attach certificate immediately after the B-BBEE declaration
3. If Level 1 or 2: highlight in a text box as competitive advantage
4. If using an affidavit: ensure signed and dated within 12 months
