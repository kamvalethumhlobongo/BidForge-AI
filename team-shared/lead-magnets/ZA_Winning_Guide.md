## 1. Understanding the SA Government Tender Landscape

South Africa's public procurement system is governed by three key pieces of legislation:

| Legislation | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Public Finance Management Act (PFMA)** | Governs national and provincial procurement |
| **Municipal Finance Management Act (MFMA)** | Governs municipal procurement |
| **Preferential Procurement Policy Framework Act (PPPFA)** | Governs preference point scoring and B-BBEE |

Every government tender in SA follows one of two preference point systems. Understanding which one applies to your bid is the first step to winning.

## 2. The Two Scoring Systems: 80/20 vs 90/10

### Which System Applies?

| Contract Value | System | Price Split | B-BBEE Split |
|---|---|---|---|
| R1,000,000 or less | **80/20** | 80 points | 20 points |
| More than R1,000,000 | **90/10** | 90 points | 10 points |

### How 80/20 Scoring Works

**Formula:** Price Points = 80 × (1 - (Your Price - Lowest Price) / Lowest Price)

**B-BBEE Points (80/20):**

| Level | Points |
|---|---|
| Level 1 | 20 |
| Level 2 | 18 |
| Level 3 | 14 |
| Level 4 | 12 |
| Level 5 | 8 |
| Level 6 | 6 |
| Level 7 | 4 |
| Level 8 | 2 |
| Non-compliant | 0 |

### How 90/10 Scoring Works

**Formula:** Price Points = 90 × (1 - (Your Price - Lowest Price) / Lowest Price)

**B-BBEE Points (90/10):**

| Level | Points |
|---|---|
| Level 1 | 10 |
| Level 2 | 9 |
| Level 3 | 8 |
| Level 4 | 6 |
| Level 5 | 4 |
| Level 6 | 3 |
| Level 7 | 2 |
| Level 8 | 1 |
| Non-compliant | 0 |

### Real Example: Tender Worth R5,000,000 (90/10 System)

| Factor | Company A | Company B | Company C |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | R5,000,000 | R4,500,000 | R4,200,000 |
| B-BBEE Level | Level 1 | Level 4 | Non-compliant |
| Price Points | 72.86 | 87.86 | **90.00** |
| B-BBEE Points | **10** | 6 | 0 |
| **Total** | **82.86** | **93.86** | **90.00** |

**Result:** Company B wins despite not having the lowest price or the highest B-BBEE level.

**Key Insight:** The sweet spot is combining a competitive price (within 5-15% of lowest) with a strong B-BBEE level (Level 1-4).

## 3. The Top 5 Disqualifiers in SA Tenders

### Disqualifier #1: Tax Clearance Not Compliant

**Why it happens:** Your SARS tax clearance status shows "Not Compliant" on CSD.

**How to avoid:** Before every bid, log into CSD and verify your tax status. If it shows "Not Compliant," resolve with SARS before bidding. This can take 2-8 weeks.

**BidForge AI tip:** We include a CSD status check as step 1 of every ZA tender process.

### Disqualifier #2: B-BBEE Certificate Issues

**Why it happens:** Certificate expired, wrong level claimed, or affidavit not valid.

**How to avoid:** 
- Set a reminder 90 days before expiry
- Ensure certificate is from a SANAS-accredited agency
- EMEs using affidavits must have them signed before a Commissioner of Oaths

**BidForge AI tip:** We track certificate expiry dates and flag renewals automatically.

### Disqualifier #3: Missing Mandatory Declarations

**Why it happens:** Standard tender documents include 8-15 declarations (conflict of interest, no collusion, local content, etc.). Missing one = disqualification.

**How to avoid:** Print every page of the tender document. Tick each declaration as you complete it. Have a second person verify.

**BidForge AI tip:** Our tender checklist includes all standard SA declarations.

### Disqualifier #4: Non-Compliant Pricing Format

**Why it happens:** You altered the pricing schedule, used a different format, or left cells blank.

**How to avoid:** 
- Use EXACTLY the format provided
- Don't change formulas in Excel schedules
- Fill every cell; write "N/A" where not applicable
- Double-check VAT inclusion/exclusion

**BidForge AI tip:** We cross-check pricing schedules against tender requirements before submission.

### Disqualifier #5: CIDB Grading Mismatch (Construction)

**Why it happens:** Your CIDB grading class is too low for the contract value.

**How to avoid:**

| Contract Value | Minimum CIDB Grade Required |
|---|---|
| Up to R500,000 | 1 CE / 1 GB |
| R500,000 - R2M | 2 CE / 2 GB |
| R2M - R5M | 3 CE / 3 GB |
| R5M - R10M | 4 CE / 4 GB |
| R10M+ | 5 CE / 5 GB+ |

## 6. Key Differences: SA vs Australian Tenders

| Aspect | South Africa | Australia |
|---|---|---|
| **Empowerment Scoring** | B-BBEE (0-25 points) | Indigenous participation (varies) |
| **Central Registration** | CSD (mandatory) | AusTender (varies by state) |
| **Tax Clearance** | SARS PIN (real-time check) | ATO (less frequently verified) |
| **Construction Grading** | CIDB (mandatory) | State-based prequalification |
| **Local Content** | Designated sectors (mandatory) | Local preference (varies) |
| **Price Formula** | Fixed: 80/20 or 90/10 | Varies by tender |
| **Submission** | eTender portal or physical | Various portals |
